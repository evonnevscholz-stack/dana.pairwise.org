Our Journey

From a simple idea to a growing movement

Pairwise Shop began in 2020 when our founders, Sarah and Michael, returned from a trip to Southeast Asia with a renewed perspective on consumption. They witnessed firsthand both the incredible craftsmanship of local artisans and the challenges they faced in accessing fair markets.

What started as a small online store featuring handmade goods from their travels has grown into a curated marketplace connecting conscious consumers with ethical producers across 15 countries. Today, we work with over 50 artisan communities and small-scale producers who share our commitment to sustainability and social responsibility.

Our name "Pairwise" reflects our core philosophy: creating meaningful, one-to-one connections between makers and buyers, and pairing each purchase with positive impact.
